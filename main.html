// WHAT IS OMORA AI

A Next-Gen AI
Image Engine on Telegram

Omora AI leverages the Realistic Vision V3.0 VAE Stable Diffusion model to transform text prompts into photorealistic, high-quality images — all through a simple Telegram bot interface. No GPU setup required on your end.

Built with a secure user verification system, personal balance management, and fully customizable generation parameters, Omora AI gives you studio-grade control in your pocket.

// DATA & PRIVACY

Your Data,
Handled with Care

Omora AI is designed with a minimal-data philosophy. We collect only what is technically necessary to operate the service — and nothing more.

All user data is stored locally on the server running the bot. No third-party analytics, no tracking pixels, no data brokers — ever.

🆔
WHAT WE COLLECT
We collect your Telegram User ID, age verification status (18+ or not), account balance, generation settings, and your prompt history with corresponding image filenames. No personal names, emails, or phone numbers are stored by the bot.
🗄️
HOW IT IS STORED
Data is stored in plaintext files within the Omora-Ai/ directory on the host server. Each user has an isolated folder (Omora-Ai/[user_id]/) containing their settings and generated images. Data is never transmitted to external services.
🔒
YOUR GENERATED IMAGES
Generated images are saved server-side with a random unique filename. They are sent directly to your Telegram account. The developer does not actively monitor or view individual user images. Prompt history is stored to maintain your creative record.
🚫
WHAT WE DO NOT DO
We do not sell your data. We do not share data with advertisers. We do not train models on your prompts or images. We do not log your Telegram messages beyond what is required by the bot framework to process commands.
